# cryptews-cryptoPlataform
Cryptews: Your All-in-One Crypto Companion

Cryptews is your go-to website for effortlessly keeping up with the exciting world of cryptocurrencies. With its user-friendly interface and powerful features, it's the perfect tool to stay informed and make informed decisions in the crypto market.

There are 3 main functionalities on the website, they are:

<li>Real-Time Market Prices: Get up-to-the-minute updates on cryptocurrency prices from various exchanges. Whether you're tracking Bitcoin, Ethereum, or any other digital asset, Cryptews has you covered.</li>
<br>
<img width="1276" alt="Screenshot 2023-11-16 at 14 46 20" src="https://github.com/13xpe/cryptews-cryptoPlataform/assets/124820216/356d022c-c12b-4078-baf1-e5057096cc07">
</br><br></br>
<li>Latest Market Fear: Stay in the know with the latest market fear index. Basically for this feature we gathered an API related to the fear index related to bitcoin that will, eventually move all cryptocurrencies.</li>
<br>
<img width="1228" alt="Screenshot 2023-11-16 at 14 49 00" src="https://github.com/13xpe/cryptews-cryptoPlataform/assets/124820216/5c96ac14-7c13-4015-8ed3-f9467f411309">
</br><br></br>
<li>Built-In Converter: Easily convert your dollars to BTC (the main cryptocurrency that runs the crypto coin market) with our built-in converter. Whether you're curious about the value of your dollar's budget in Bitcoin, Ethereum, or other 3 cryptos with huge market cap.</li>
